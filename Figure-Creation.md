## Procedure
1. Determine Vitality
	* Throw 2d twice and record the higher number as your Vitality
2. Select a Kin 
	* Choose 1 skill from the cultural skills list and set it to rank 1.  
3. Select a Job 
	* Record "basic training" skill at Rank 1
	* Looking at your job's Term Benefits Table: Roll 1d6 to determine the row and select a 1 skill from column 1, 2 or 3, add a rank to the chosen skill, and repeat two more times.
	* Using the same table: Roll 1d6 to determine the row and select 1 benefit from column 4 or 5, repeat two more times.
4. Finishing Touches 
	* Purchase any extra gear you wish with your starting coin
